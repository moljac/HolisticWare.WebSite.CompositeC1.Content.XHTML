HolisticWare - System And Software
Design, Development and Integration

HolisticWare is an IT company from Zagreb, Croatia, founded in 2007-09 specialized in System and Software Design, Development and Integration.

Vision

The company vision to bring modern "state-of-the-art", but still affordable technology to each home and small business and to transform the way private users and small businesses acquire and operate their IT and communications systems based upon open communications through the application of recent breakthroughs in technology and science.

Reach towards Customer Client

One of the goals is to help our customers them to reduce implementation and operational costs of IT systems in general, while omitting hiding of "secrets" before them. Thus making customers equal and competent partner in all solutions provided.

Mission

Company mission is to bring technological edge to private users and small business and to make them able enjoy advantages of high-tech in everyday living, at home, at work, practicing hobbies such as sports etc.

HolisticWare provides

Since its founding HolisticWare has proved itself as a reliable and trustworthy partner to more than 40 small businesses and several ISPs. List of implemented solutions is growing bigger and bigger from day to day.

International aspects

After several years, HolisticWare team has managed a breakthrough on international scene proving expertise and knowledge abroad.
