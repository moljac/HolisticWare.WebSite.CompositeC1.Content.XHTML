2013-09-15 Another Xamarin component MarkDownDeep ported and packaged by HolisticWare Team accepted on Xamarin Component Store

One more component ported to Xamarin.iOS and Xamarin.Android by HolisticWare team was accepted and approved.
The component is MarkDownDeep library which enables MarkDown to MarkUp (HTML) transformations.
