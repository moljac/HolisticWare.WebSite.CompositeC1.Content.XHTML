2013-08-19 First component SharpSerializer ported and packaged by HolisticWare Team accepted on Xamarin Component Store

First component ported to Xamarin.iOS and Xamarin.Android by HolisticWare team was accepted and approved.
The component is SharpSerializer library which enables binary and xml serialization.

More info:
