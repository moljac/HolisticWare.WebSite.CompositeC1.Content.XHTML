PhActEx

Intro

Physical fitness tests are intrusive, meaning ...

PhActEx (pronounced like FactEx - Fact Expert) is a software suite under development for usage used in amateur/recreational or professional sports or even medical recovery. PhActEx is a help for acquisition and analysis of data for relevant parameters in kinesiology.

Features include:

  • data acquistion (collecting of data relevant for physical activity or sports -measurements)
  • analysis (mining, classification etc...) of relevant parameters in kinesiology
  • numerical (mathematical) modeling of data and functionalities
  • graphical modeling (biomechanics)
  • multimedial analysis (motorics, kinemechanics) (motion picture - video and still image - picture analysis)
  • programming and planning of the training process ()
  • supervision/control of the training process
