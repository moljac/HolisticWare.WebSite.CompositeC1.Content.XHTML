Optimizations

HolisticWare team with its experience offers help, consulting and services in 2 types of optimizations in web development:

  • Search Engine Optimizations (SEO)
    Very important for marketing (free or cheap marketing on the web) to drive the site closer to the #1 on search engines (Google especially). To gain more clicks, visitors, users and possibly revenue.
  • Speed (Page Speed optimizations)
    Important for user experience (UX) to get to the information as soon as possible. World Wide Waiting drives users away and thus possible customers.
