  1. Flash stuff will not work on mobile devices (2012-08-22) iOS [iPhone, iPad] and Android
  2. Videos with multimedia (audio) under DRM will not play on mobile devices if hosted on YouTube. Namely Google analyzes file (streams) and searches for patterns. On PC they show ADS, but on mobile devices not, so reproduction of multimedia content with DRM is disabled/prohibited.
    Workaround: embed google video on the site!
