Control Over Gmail IMAP Folders

Default Gmail IMAP folders cannot be removed or set hidden from appearing in IMAP which can be very annoying, especially if u use some kind of search application (Spotlight …) on your computer, which can in turn produce duplicated results, one from your Labels folder and another from All Mail folder.

Well interested thing is that Google is offering us a solution for that problem, in form of Google Labs, specifically Advanced IMAP Control!

First thing, log into your Gmail account, go to Settings (right corner), and choose Labs.

Find Advanced IMAP Controls and set to Enable.

Go to your Inbox, choose Labels, manage Labels and here you go, you now have the option to remove every folder from IMAP!
