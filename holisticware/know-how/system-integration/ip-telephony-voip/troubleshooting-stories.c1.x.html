Cisco phones - not working

C: Customer
HW: one of us

Phone call from C (200+ phones)

  1. C: New Cisco phones are not working?
  2. HW: Meaning?
  3. C: All Linksys phones work, but new Cisco do not!
  4. HW: What does it say on the not-working-phone? Initializing network? Namely, Holisticware setup is that each phone is bound to its MAC in DHCP. Did You chang something?
  5. C: No.
  6. HW: What is IP address of one of those phones?
  7. C: 192.168.x.x
  8. HW: There must be another DHCP server on vlan!
  9. C: No way. Why only new phones? Do I need to update firmware?
  10. HW: I suppose that new phones have faster lan (1GB nic) and better processor and pickup DHCP offers from this ghost server faster than old Linksys...
  11. C: OK. I'll try with my laptop. Call U later...

Few miutes later...

  1. C: It was another DHCP server - guys from other department have just plugged it in without notification!

Moral of the story: Whoz da Bos?
